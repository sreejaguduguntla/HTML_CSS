Bangalore

Bangalore, the capital of Karnataka, is a vibrant city known for its rich cultural heritage and bustling urban life. The city is home to beautiful parks like Lalbagh and a thriving arts scene. It's also famous for its temples, including the iconic Bangalore Palace.

Bangalore has emerged as a major hub for IT and business services, with numerous multinational corporations setting up operations here. The city's blend of traditional charm and modern development makes it a unique destination in South India.
