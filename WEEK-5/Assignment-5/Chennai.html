Chennai

Chennai, the capital of Tamil Nadu, is a vibrant city known for its rich cultural heritage and bustling urban life. The city is home to beautiful beaches like Marina Beach and a thriving arts scene. It's also famous for its temples, including the iconic Sri Parthasarathy Temple.

Chennai has emerged as a major hub for IT and business services, with numerous multinational corporations setting up operations here. The city's blend of traditional charm and modern development makes it a unique destination in South India.
